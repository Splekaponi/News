Elimination for Damath and Math Quiz

On October 10, 2024, the students of Pines City National High School (PCNHS) competed in the school elimination round to move on to the district level for Damath and Math Quiz. Students selected to compete exchanged intelligence, vying for a spot at the next level.

De Guzman, Alynna Shane S and Visca, Tristine Allain D, from 7-D, coached by Ma'am Gam-ad, Jocelyn Q, won 3rd place for the elimination. These people are coached by the respectful teacher/coaches who are willing to make the student more intellectual and competitive. They motivate them to do better and be confident in competing.

Recognized as one of the foremost competitors in the Grade 7 Math Quiz, Pascua, Epefanio A, candidly shares his experience: "Throughout the intense Math competition, I found myself grappling most with the complexities of Geometry and the intricacies of Algebraic Expressions. Each of the talented competitors is guided by their esteemed coaches and educators, whose unwavering dedication and profound wisdom inspire and uplift their students. These mentors instill not only knowledge but also an incredible sense of motivation and self-belief that drives the students to reach new heights. The passion and commitment these coaches bring to their role are truly immeasurable, and for this, we are deeply grateful. Their encouragement is the silent force behind every success, and we owe much of the students' growth to their nurturing hands.
